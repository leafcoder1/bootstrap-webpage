About this project

  • Project Members:1
  • Production Period:3 weeks
  • Download: Inugamitotatakai3

Details

A game made for a school game jam in my 3rd year. This is a sequel to Inugami2. Go through the first conversation and select an option. The ending changes depending on your choices! Just like last time, there are different options from the previous games! The theme this time is summer. Once you choose to fight Inugami, the battle will begin. Like in an RPG, you and Inugami have turns, and depending on the action you choose, you can deal damage to him and recover HP!
